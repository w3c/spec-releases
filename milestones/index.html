Milestones

This document is intended to help Chairs and Team Contacts in planning their deadlines for specification milestones.

In theory, it takes around 174 days to move a specification to W3C Recommendation, without open exclusion opportunities. Pick one of your publication date and the other dates will be computed for you.

The computation takes into account the W3C Process, the W3C Patent Policy, and the transition requirements.

  • Transition request to Candidate Recommendation, wide review is done
  • , start of a 60 days exclusion opportunity, start of the Advisory Committee Review
  • Deadline for comments
  • Transition request to Proposed Recommendation, showing adequate implementation experience,
  • , deadline for the Advisory Committee Review is set
  • Last day of the 150 days exclusion opportunity
  • Last day of the 60 days exclusion opportunity
  • Last day of the Advisory Committee Review
